## KPI brief (verbatim from Andre)

Monthly report to the Sales Directors (level below MDs). Forward looking, insight-driven, PowerPoint format.

- A pipeline overview with quarterly focus (one slide per region)
- Commercial Approval overview - which deals have been approved and a list of any Land stage 3 deals with no commercial approval. Global overview (one slide) + list of candidates by region (one slide).
- Renewals tracking - what renewals are coming up this quarter, what is the value and likelihood of renewing.
- Churn Risk and trends - difficult for now, but try to build a slide of what we can get from Finance. Andre is to reach out to Alex P. The current snapshot has `finance_feed_status: pending`.
- Slipped deals analysis (root cause commentary) - start with slipped deals; root cause commentary requires reaching out to the opportunity owner. Andre will structure the outreach.

## Hard rules applied to this dashboard

1. **Calendar year only.** No FY labels. Use `April 2026` or `Q2 2026` (calendar).
2. **ACV for renewals.** Land and expand pipeline stays in ARR. This is a SimCorp methodology quirk. Canonical fields: `APTS_Renewal_ACV__c` for renewal ACV, `APTS_Opportunity_ARR__c` for land/expand ARR. **Source:** SimCorp Sales Handbook V4 slides 24-25 ("ARR is the recurring part of ACV"; renewals show only net new ARR under `APTS_Opportunity_ARR__c`, so renewal-value reporting must use the dedicated `APTS_Renewal_ACV__c` field instead).
3. **Type field is canonical for renewal / land / expand.** The `APTS_Primary_Quote_Type__c` field is stale and the org emptied its picklist entirely (verified via sobject describe - zero active picklist values). Use `Type='Renewal'`, `Type='Land'`, `Type='Expand'` on the standard `Type` field.
4. **No em-dashes** anywhere in widget labels. Use hyphens, periods, or rephrase.
5. **No gauges or donuts** in widget visuals. Bullet charts and ranked bars only.
6. **Forecast accuracy is on Sales Directors as well as Sales Ops.** Source: Sales Handbook V4 slide 17 ("Managing Directors, Sales Directors and Heads of Customer Success are also responsible for the forecast accuracy and adjustments"). Report 1 includes a forecast accuracy snapshot widget (#16) sourced from Pipeline Inspection native, not just Report 2.

## Open questions

Field-name questions have been resolved via sobject describe on 2026-04-06 and against the Sales Handbook V4 on 2026-04-07. Remaining semantic questions:

1. ~~**Renewals "likelihood"** - is that opportunity `Probability` (%), a stage-based proxy (StageName maps to rough %), or a custom field?~~ **RESOLVED 2026-04-07** via Sales Handbook V4 slide 17: standard SF `Probability` field, default stage-derived, owner-overridable. Used by widget 11.
2. **Churn placeholder wording** - what does widget 13 say while the Finance feed is pending? _Current assumption: "Awaiting Finance feed (Alex P)"._
3. **Slipped deals root cause column** - opportunity-owner commentary is pending. Until then, is the column blank, "Pending", or dropped? _Current assumption: column present but blank, label "Root cause (pending)"._
4. **"Quarterly focus" scope** - current calendar quarter only, rolling 4 quarters, or current plus next? _Current assumption: current calendar quarter only._
5. **Does "Middle East & Africa" deserve its own slide?** The 7 distinct `Sales_Region__c` values are UKI, CE, NE, SWE, NAM, APAC, MEA. The current spec groups MEA with APAC (4 slides total: global, EMEA, NAM, APAC+MEA) to match the existing CRMA dashboard convention. _Current assumption: MEA grouped with APAC._
6. **Slipped deals canonical source.** Spec now declares Pipeline Inspection native as canonical for widgets 14-15. Need to confirm with Sales Ops whether the deck slides should be PI screenshots, PI link-outs, or programmatic SOQL fetches against `OpportunityFieldHistory` to recompute PI's slipped metric in-deck. _Current assumption: PI screenshots or link-outs, no programmatic recomputation._
7. **"Missing approval" semantics** - the spec uses `Stage_20_Approval__c=false` for "Land Stage 3 missing approval". An alternative interpretation is `Stage_20_Approval__c=false AND Submit_for_Stage_20_Review__c=false` (neither submitted nor approved). _Current assumption: just the approval flag; submission state is not checked._ Per Sales Handbook V4 slide 12, the Go/No-Go gate fires for ALL Land deals upstream of stage 3, so `Stage_20_Approval__c=false` at stage 3 is the right defect signal regardless of submission state.
8. **Forecast accuracy widget shape.** Widget 16 declares Pipeline Inspection native as canonical but does not yet specify the exact PI list view or metric config that feeds it. Phase 2 source-contract work will probe `PipelineInspectionListView` for active configurations and pin the right one.
