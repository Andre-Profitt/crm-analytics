# Q1 Handling Rules

## Goal

Show what was promised versus what was delivered without overstating the confidence of the promise baseline.

## Allowed

- Q1 actual won count and ARR
- Q1 actual lost count and ARR
- director-scoped slipped-deal count and ARR
- director-scoped forecast-category movement summary

## Not Safe Without Qualification

- workbook `Q1 Review` forecast blocks copied from global or CRO-level ForecastingItem extracts
- pushed-deals tables that are not director-scoped

## Language Contract

Use wording like:

- `Delivered Q1 actuals were ...`
- `X deals slipped out of Q1 carrying ...`
- `The clean promise baseline is ambiguous in the workbook and should not be presented as the director commitment without qualification.`

Do not use wording like:

- `The director promised ...` unless the source is explicitly director-safe
