# Missing Slide Contract

Treat a slide as materially missing if the deck does not adequately answer one of these questions:

## Pipeline

- what does the quarter actually look like for this director?

## Commercial Approval

- which stage-3 land deals are approved, and which are still exposed?

## Renewals

- what renewal ACV is due this quarter, and how likely is it to renew?

## Q1 Review

- what did we deliver versus what slipped out?

## Slipped Deals

- where is recovery pressure concentrated, and do we have real owner commentary?

## Execution

- what other operational intelligence matters right now: coverage, hygiene, concentration, or competitive losses?
