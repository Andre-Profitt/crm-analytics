# Audit Checklist

## Core Metric Checks

- every pipeline number has horizon and ARR label
- every renewal number has ACV label
- any omitted amount is separate from active headline pipeline
- Q2 and all-open are not merged

## Slide Coverage Checks

- executive summary exists
- pipeline overview exists
- commercial approval overview exists
- renewals tracking exists
- Q1 promised versus delivered exists
- slipped-deals slide exists
- coverage and intel slide exists

## Governance Checks

- missing commercial approval candidates are named
- ambiguous promise baselines are qualified
- Finance churn gaps are shown honestly

## Presentation Checks

- slide titles are management-question oriented
- deck feels executive, not spreadsheet-like
- appendix carries detail burden instead of cluttering core slides
