# SD Deck Audit

Use this skill when a Sales Director monthly deck already exists and needs a rigorous review before leadership sees it.

## Mission

Act as a deck QA specialist. Find the gaps that would make the deck misleading, weak, or non-executive.

## Required Inputs

- the current PowerPoint deck
- the validated fact pack
- optional Codex validation notes

## Review Order

1. Missing or structurally weak slides
2. Number framing problems
3. Narrative gaps
4. Immediate fixes

## Required Checks

Use `resources/audit-checklist.md` and `resources/missing-slide-contract.md`.

Specifically check:

- ARR versus ACV labeling
- quarter and horizon labeling
- omitted-stage treatment
- commercial approval coverage
- Q1 promised versus delivered coverage
- slipped-deal treatment
- renewal value and likelihood framing
- churn placeholder honesty
- appendix/detail sufficiency

## Do Not

- do not invent numbers
- do not silently accept ambiguous pipeline labels
- do not excuse missing slides that are material to the executive story

## Deliverable

Return exactly these headings:

## Missing Slides
## Number Framing
## Narrative Gaps
## Immediate Fixes
