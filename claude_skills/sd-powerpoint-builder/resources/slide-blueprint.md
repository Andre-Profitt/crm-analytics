# Slide Blueprint

This blueprint is for one Sales Director monthly report.

## 1. Cover

- director name
- territory
- snapshot date
- subtitle: monthly pipeline and operating review

## 2. Executive Summary

- 3 leadership bullets only
- highest-signal current-quarter read
- explicit mention of the strongest risk or exception

## 3. Pipeline Overview

- quarterly focus first
- show `All Open`, `FY26`, and `Q2` only if they can be clearly distinguished
- include coverage and top opportunities only if they help explain the quarter

## 4. Commercial Approval Overview

- current approved state
- named missing-approval candidates
- stage-3 land governance gaps

## 5. Renewals Tracking

- upcoming renewals this quarter
- total renewal `ACV (€ converted)`
- renewal likelihood or risk where actually present

## 6. Q1 Promised vs Delivered

- what delivered in Q1
- what slipped out of Q1
- what is ambiguous in the promise baseline

## 7. Slipped Deals and Root-Cause Gap

- factual slipped-deal pattern now
- biggest repeat-push or aging pressure
- if owner commentary is missing, explicitly show the commentary gap

## 8. Coverage and Critical Intel

- forecast mix, hygiene pressure, rep concentration, competitive losses, or other material execution signal

## 9. Appendix or Proof Slide

- opportunity detail, named exceptions, or source notes
- only if useful for the management audience

## Style Contract

- executive, sparse, and directional
- one message per slide
- avoid table-heavy walls unless the appendix truly requires detail
